# TECH-PRO SOLUTIONS: CUSTOMER SERVICE KNOWLEDGE BASE

## 1. Shipping and Delivery Policies
- Standard Shipping: Takes 3-5 business days. Costs $5.99 or free for orders over $50.
- Express Shipping: Takes 1-2 business days. Costs $15.00.
- International Shipping: Available to over 50 countries. Delivery takes 7-14 business days.
- Tracking: Customers can track their order via the 'My Orders' section on our website or through the link sent in the shipping confirmation email.

## 2. Returns and Refunds
- Policy: Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase if they are in original condition and packaging.
- Process: To start a return, visit tech-pro.com/returns and enter your order number.
- Refunds: Once we receive the item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
- Non-returnable Items: Software downloads, personalized items, and clearance 'Final Sale' products.

## 3. Account Security and Support
- Password Reset: Users can reset passwords by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page. An OTP will be sent to their registered email.
- Account Lock: Accounts are locked for 30 minutes after 5 failed login attempts for security.
- Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): We support 2FA via Google Authenticator or SMS. Setup is found in Account Settings > Security.

## 4. Technical Troubleshooting
- Device Not Connecting: Ensure the device is charged. Reset the network settings on the device and try re-pairing.
- App Crashing: Check for the latest update in the App Store or Play Store. If persistent, clear the app cache and restart your smartphone.
- Warranty: All hardware products come with a 1-year limited manufacturer's warranty.

## 5. Contacting Human Support
- Live Chat: Available 24/7 on our website.
- Email: support@tech-pro.com (Response time: 24 hours).
- Phone: 1-800-TECH-PRO (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 6 PM EST).
